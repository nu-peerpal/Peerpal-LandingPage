Who Are We?

We are a team of open-minded and inquisitive students who are passionate about technology and education working on a peer grading platform project headed by Professor Jason Hartline at Northwestern University. Our developers are all students at Northwestern who strive to achieve their best on this project while also gaining in-depth knowledge of web development.
